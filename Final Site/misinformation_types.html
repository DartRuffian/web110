The Different Types of Misinformation

There are multiple types of misinformation, each with a different purpose for their audience. Some types of misinformation influence people accidentally, and did not have the intent to misinform, while others are made exclusively to trick its audience. These seven types listed below are ordered from least malicious to most malicious.

1. Satire / Parody

Does not intentionally try to harm others, but has the potential to fool.

2. False Connection

When titles, captions, or media does not support the given content.

3. Misleading Content

Using information with the intent to mislead to frame an issue or person.

4. False Context

When content that is true is shared with false information in the same context.

5. Impostor Content

Impersonating trusted sources to make their content seem more believable.

6. Manipulated Content

Correct information or media that has been manipulated to deceive its audience.

7. Fabricated Content

Original content that is completely false and specifically designed to trick and do harm.
